Instructions on how to play Gothonweb

Please read below and play the game!

Demo on Heroku

click to play on heroku

To play game on command line

  • clone the repository to your local repository
  • and on your command line run the command below
  • $ ruby gothonweb.rb

To play game in the local browser

  • clone the repository to your local repository
  • and on your command line run the command below
  • $ ruby gothonweb.rb
  • Try to input gubbage and press enter
  • This will lead to death and immediately the cmd will establish connection to your browser
  • Once the connection is made..open your browser and the url below
  • http://localhost:4567
  • The press enter to play

The Game

Once you are in the Central Corridor, or the Starting point

  • you will be prompted and what you are supposed to do is just run the command below
    • $ tell a joke
  • The command above will take you to the Laser Weapon Armory room
  • Once prompted enter the code below to open the container and take the bomb out
    • $ 0132
  • This will take you to the bridge room where you have two options whether
    • 1. throw the bomb or 2. slowly place the bomb
  • If succeded you will be taken to the escape pod to completely escape if you take the right pod
    • $ 2
  • If you take pod 2 actually is the right pod and you win! otherwise you die.
  • Wrong options for the prompt to any of the stage above, will cause your death.
  • Either won or lost, before the game terminate it will open the connection to your browser so as you can play the game on your browser
